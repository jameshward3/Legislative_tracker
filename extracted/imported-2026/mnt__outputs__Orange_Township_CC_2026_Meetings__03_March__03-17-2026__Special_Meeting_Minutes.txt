RESOLUTION
155A-2026: A resolution to hold an Executive (Closed) Session to discuss Litigation Matters.
PURPOSE: The purpose of this meeting is to discuss Litigation Matters.
A motion to adopt Resolution 155A-2026 was made by Councilmember Clifford R. Ross and seconded by Council Vice
President Weldon M. Montague, III.
Roll Call – Yeas: Hon. Tency A. Eason, Hon. Quantavia L. Hilbert, Hon. Weldon M. Montague, III, Hon. Clifford R. Ross, and
Hon. Adrienne K. Wooten. Absent: Hon. Kerry J. Coley and Hon. Jamie B. Summers-Johnson. Motion Adopted.
Entered into Executive Closed Session at 6:12pm.
EXECUTIVE SESSION SUMMARY
City Attorney Gracia R. Montilus, Esq. provided an update on pending litigation matters. He explained the division of legal
work between the City's internal legal staff and outside counsel. Less complex matters are handled in-house by Deputy City
Attorneys Avram White, Esq., Aaron Mizrahi, Esq., and Attorney Alex Cirroco, Esq., while more complex cases are assigned
to outside law firms. The complex cases are currently distributed between two firms, with approximately ten (10) cases
assigned to one firm and five (5) cases assigned to the other. City Attorney Montilus also reviewed the current status of
these matters and discussed strategies and plans moving forward.
