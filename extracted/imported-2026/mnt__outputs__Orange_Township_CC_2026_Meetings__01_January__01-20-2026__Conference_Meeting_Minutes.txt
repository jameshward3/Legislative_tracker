MATTERS FOR DISCUSSION
Health Update
Vincent DeFilippo, Health Officer, provided an update on current public health concerns including the upcoming World Cup,
elevated infectious disease activity (measles, COVID-19), recent health advisories and recalls, and a newly identified
outbreak involving a rare infection. He emphasized that vaccination remains the most effective tool in preventing the spread
of infectious diseases.
Fire Department Update
Fire Chief Derrick Brown provided a brief update on fire safety and preparedness ahead of the upcoming snowstorm. He
asked residents to shovel snow away from fire hydrants, provided safety tips for portable space heaters, and warned
residents not to use ovens to heat their homes. He acknowledged recent promotions including new captains.
