City of Orange Township – City Council
 Conference Meeting Minutes – February 3, 2026
MINUTES CITY COUNCIL – CITY OF ORANGE TOWNSHIP, NEW JERSEY
THIS IS A CONFERENCE MEETING OF THE MUNICIPAL COUNCIL OF THE CITY OF ORANGE TOWNSHIP HELD AT
CITY HALL, 29 NORTH DAY STREET, ORANGE, NEW JERSEY ON TUESDAY, FEBRUARY 3, 2026 AT 7:01PM.
ROLL CALL
Present: Councilmember Kerry J. Coley, East Ward; Councilmember Tency A. Eason, North Ward; Councilmember
Quantavia L. Hilbert, West Ward; Council Vice President Weldon M. Montague, III, At-Large; Councilmember Clifford R.
Ross, At-Large; Councilmember Jamie B. Summers-Johnson, South Ward (Arrived at 7:15pm).
Also Present: Trisha A. Scipio, Deputy Municipal Clerk; Chris M. Hartwyk, Business Administrator; Derrick Brown, Fire
Chief; Nile S. Clements, Chief Financial Officer; Brian Mooney, Police Chief; Gracia R. Montilus, Esq. City Attorney (Arrived
at 7:10pm); Marlin G. Townes, Legislative Research Officer.
Absent: Council President Adrienne K. Wooten, At-Large.
The Essex County Update was moved to after the discussion of legislation on the agenda.
RESOLUTIONS
75-2026: A resolution Qualifying Vendors for Legislative Research Officer Services for 2026. Councilmember Ross asked
for explanation – this resolution accepts the Qualifying Vendors from the RFP's submitted (only one submission).
77-2026: A resolution authorizing the cancellation of Tax Sale Certificates due to inaccurate utility billing caused by
malfunctioning meters.
78-2026: AMENDED – Resolution authorizing the cancellation of Tax Sale Certificate No. 24-00194 and refund of $5,316.26
for subsequent payments and $4,683.48 for applicable fees, penalties, and interest.
80-2026: A resolution authorizing acceptance of a grant totaling an additional $18,500.00 from NJACCHO for the Enhancing
Local Public Health Infrastructure Grant (earmarked for Part-Time Health Officer).
82-2026: A resolution authorizing the extension of contract with Pennoni Associates, Inc. for professional engineering and
design services for NJDOT 2023 LTPF Roadway Improvements, not to exceed $93,368.60.
85-2026: A resolution awarding a contract for CDBG'24 municipal building main door replacement to HM Company, LLC,
not to exceed $69,000.00.
86-2026: A resolution authorizing Change Order No. 1 to the NJDOT 2024 Roadway Improvements contract with ADG
Contracting Corp for additional PVC Handrails at 603 Beach Street, new total not to exceed $626,636.99.
89-2026: A resolution authorizing an addendum to contract with Pugliese Pool Service, Inc. increasing amount by
$5,850.00, new total not to exceed $79,014.00.
90-2026: A resolution awarding contract to Downes Tree Service Co., Inc. for Tree Removal and trimming services, not to
exceed $50,000.00.
91-2026: A resolution awarding contract to Denville Painting Inc. for traffic striping on roadways, not to exceed $15,000.00.
94-2026: A resolution congratulating students of the Orange Public School District for achieving Honor Roll status
(Councilmember Ross wants the names of the students for congratulatory letters).
95-2026 through 100-2026: AMENDED – Various resolutions honoring individuals during Black History Month, amended to
include the entire Council.
Essex County Update
