City of Orange Township – City Council
 Conference Meeting Minutes – January 6, 2026
MINUTES CITY COUNCIL – CITY OF ORANGE TOWNSHIP, NEW JERSEY
THIS IS A CONFERENCE MEETING OF THE CITY COUNCIL OF THE CITY OF ORANGE TOWNSHIP HELD AT CITY
HALL, 29 NORTH DAY STREET, ORANGE, NEW JERSEY ON TUESDAY, JANUARY 6, 2026 AT 7:08PM.
ROLL CALL
Present: Councilmember Kerry J. Coley, East Ward; Councilmember Quantavia L. Hilbert, West Ward; Councilmember
Jamie B. Summers-Johnson, South Ward; Council President Adrienne K. Wooten, At-Large.
Also Present: Trisha A. Scipio, Deputy Municipal Clerk; Chris M. Hartwyk, Business Administrator; Derrick Brown, Fire
Chief; Nile Clements, Chief Financial Officer; Brian Mooney, Police Chief (Arrived at 7:13pm); Gracia R. Montilus, Esq. City
Attorney; Marlin G. Townes, Legislative Research Officer.
Absent: Councilmember Tency A. Eason, North Ward; Councilmember Clifford R. Ross, At-Large; Council Vice President
Weldon M. Montague, III, At-Large.
The Deputy Clerk announced a Moment of Silence in honor of Councilmember Tency A. Eason in light of the loss of her
loved one.
MATTERS FOR DISCUSSION
Essex County Update
Reginald Bledsoe, Essex County Liaison announced upcoming events including: A Job Fair for seasonal positions at Essex
County Turtle Back Zoo on January 31st, February 21st, and March 7th; The 19th Annual Project Homeless Connect Day
on Wednesday, January 29th at Essex County College Gym.
Orange Education Presentation
Members of the Orange Education Foundation and Orange Board of Education addressed the Council regarding the
Radium Girls Memorial Project. Terry Russo (OEF) spoke about a $10,000 matching grant, noting Orange is the only city of
three involved that does not presently have a memorial to the Radium Girls.
ORDINANCES – SECOND READING
74-2025: An ordinance of the Municipal Council of the City of Orange Township to approve a tax exemption expiring
November 1, 2055 and authorize a Financial Agreement with Peek Lincoln Urban Renewal LLC. This Ordinance will be
postponed to the January 20th meeting.
ORDINANCES – INTRODUCTION / FIRST READING
There were no Ordinances of Introduction / First Reading.
RESOLUTIONS
3-2026: A resolution authorizing a contract with Nishuane Group LLC for professional planning consultant services for 12
months commencing January 1, 2026, not to exceed $150,000.00. This resolution was amended – the last Whereas clause
was amended to include language referencing the Temporary Budget Account.
9-2026: A resolution authorizing the adoption of Current Fund and Water/Sewer Utility Fund Temporary Budget
Appropriations for the beginning of 2026. This resolution was amended to include the Parking Utility Budget 2026.
ADJOURNMENT
A motion to adjourn was made by Councilmember Jamie B. Summers-Johnson and seconded by Councilmember Kerry J.
Coley. Motion Adopted. The meeting was adjourned at 7:26pm.
Approved: January 20, 2026
